NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4060 Ti

The GeForce RTX 4060 Ti 8 GB is a high-end graphics card built on NVIDIA’s 5 nm Ada Lovelace architecture. It features 4352 CUDA cores, 136 tensor cores, and 34 RT cores. With 8 GB GDDR6 memory on a 128-bit bus, it delivers great performance for 1080p and 1440p gaming, and supports ray tracing and AI-accelerated features. It operates with a boost clock of 2535 MHz and a power draw of 160W. Display outputs include 1x HDMI 2.1 and 3x DisplayPort 1.4a. The dual-slot design ensures compatibility with most modern builds.
