NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5070

The GeForce RTX 5070 is a high-end GPU launched on March 4, 2025. Based on the GB205-300-A1 GPU built using a 5nm process, it supports DirectX 12 Ultimate for future-ready performance.

It features 6,144 shaders, 192 texture mapping units, 192 tensor cores, 48 RT cores, and 80 ROPs. It comes with 12 GB GDDR7 VRAM on a 192-bit interface, running at an effective 28 Gbps.

The GPU runs at 2325 MHz (boosting up to 2512 MHz), draws 250W via a 16-pin connector, and outputs to 1x HDMI 2.1b and 3x DisplayPort 2.1b. With a compact 245mm x 115mm x 40mm dual-slot design, it's perfect for performance builds.
