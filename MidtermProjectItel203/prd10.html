NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5090

The GeForce RTX 5090 is an enthusiast-class GPU released on January 30, 2025. Based on the 5nm GB202-300-A1 processor, it supports DirectX 12 Ultimate, ensuring compatibility with the latest and upcoming games.

It boasts 21,760 shading units, 680 TMUs, 176 ROPs, 680 tensor cores, and 170 RT cores. Backed by 32GB of GDDR7 memory on a 512-bit bus, it runs at 1750 MHz (28 Gbps effective), while the core clocks between 2017 MHz and 2407 MHz boost.

With a dual-slot design, the card consumes up to 575W via a 16-pin power connector. Outputs include 1x HDMI 2.1b and 3x DisplayPort 2.1b. It connects via PCIe 5.0 x16 and measures 304mm x 137mm x 40mm.
