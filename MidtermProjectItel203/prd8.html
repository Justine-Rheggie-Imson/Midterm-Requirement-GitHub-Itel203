NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090

The GeForce RTX 4090 is NVIDIA's flagship GPU, released on September 20, 2022. Built using the AD102-300-A1 chip with a 5nm process, it supports DirectX 12 Ultimate for modern and future-proof gaming.

It features 16,384 shading units, 512 texture mapping units, 512 tensor cores, 128 ray tracing cores, and 176 ROPs. It is paired with a massive 24 GB of GDDR6X VRAM using a 384-bit interface for unmatched bandwidth.

Clocked at 2235 MHz (boosting up to 2520 MHz), it draws 450W via a 16-pin connector. Display outputs include 1x HDMI 2.1 and 3x DisplayPort 1.4a. Its physical dimensions are 304mm x 137mm x 61mm, requiring triple-slot cooling.
