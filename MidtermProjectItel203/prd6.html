NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 Ti

The GeForce RTX 4070 Ti is an enthusiast-class GPU launched on January 3, 2023. Powered by the AD104-400-A1 chip and built on the 5 nm process, this card features 7680 CUDA cores, 240 tensor cores, and 60 ray tracing cores. It's equipped with 12 GB of GDDR6X VRAM across a 192-bit interface, with clock speeds up to 2610 MHz boost and 21 Gbps memory speed.

It supports DirectX 12 Ultimate and features a dual-slot cooling solution. Power draw is rated at 285W, using a single 16-pin connector. Display outputs include 1x HDMI 2.1 and 3x DisplayPort 1.4a. Dimensions are 285mm x 112mm x 42mm.
