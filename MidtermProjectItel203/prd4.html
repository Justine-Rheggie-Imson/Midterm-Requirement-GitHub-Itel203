NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3090 Ti

The GeForce RTX 3090 Ti is NVIDIA’s top-tier enthusiast-class graphics card, launched in 2022. Featuring 10752 CUDA cores, 336 tensor cores, and 84 ray tracing cores, it's equipped with a massive 24 GB of GDDR6X memory on a 384-bit bus. With a boost clock of 1860 MHz and a TDP of 450W, it’s built for the most demanding 4K gaming and content creation. The card also supports PCIe 4.0, 1x HDMI 2.1, and 3x DisplayPort 1.4a outputs. It comes in a triple-slot design for maximum cooling and performance.
