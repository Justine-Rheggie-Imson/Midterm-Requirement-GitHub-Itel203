NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4080

The GeForce RTX 4080 is an enthusiast-class GPU launched on September 20, 2022. Based on the AD103-300-A1 chip and manufactured with the 5 nm process, it supports DirectX 12 Ultimate, ensuring compatibility with modern and upcoming games.

The GPU includes 9728 shading units, 304 texture mapping units, 304 tensor cores, 76 ray tracing cores, and 112 ROPs. Paired with 16 GB of GDDR6X memory on a 256-bit bus, it delivers fast performance and high memory bandwidth (22.4 Gbps effective).

Operating at a base clock of 2205 MHz and boosting up to 2505 MHz, it has a max power draw of 320W via a 16-pin connector. Outputs include 1x HDMI 2.1 and 3x DisplayPort 1.4a. Dimensions: 310mm x 140mm x 61mm with a triple-slot cooling solution.
